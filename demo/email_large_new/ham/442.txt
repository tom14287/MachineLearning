the histori of medicin:

  2000 b.c. -- here, eat thi root.
  1000 a.d. -- that root is heathen. sai thi prayer.
  1850 a.d. -- that prayer is superstit. drink thi potion.
  1940 a.d. -- that potion is snake oil. swallow thi pill.
  1985 a.d. -- that pill is ineffect. take thi antibiot.
  2000 a.d. -- that antibiot is artifici. here, eat thi root.
